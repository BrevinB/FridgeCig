# FridgeCig Outreach Kit — Newsletters, Press, Creators, Podcasts

One pitch per week (see the [weekly playbook](00-master-strategy.md#5-the-weekly-playbook-25-hours-repeatable-forever)). Every pitch links a **press-specific App Store custom product page** so you can attribute coverage, and offers assets from the [press kit](#press-kit-one-pager) below.

## Target list

Work top to bottom within a tier; alternate tiers week to week. Verify current submission instructions before sending — most of these have a tips email or form.

### Tier 1 — Indie-app press & newsletters (highest hit rate for a polished niche app)
| Target | Angle | How to reach |
|--------|-------|--------------|
| MacStories | Beautifully-made niche SwiftUI app; widgets + Watch + App Intents story | tips/contact form; mention specific Apple-tech adoption |
| 9to5Mac | Quirky-app coverage, weekend app roundups | tips email |
| Cult of Mac | Loves fun/weird Apple apps | tips form |
| Indie App Spotlight–style newsletters/accounts | Solo dev, gamified niche app | submission forms; search "indie app spotlight newsletter" for current ones |
| "App of the Day"-style IG/TikTok accounts | Ready-made demo Reels they can repost | DM with a 15s demo video attached |
| Product Hunt | Not press, but a launch surface — do a "2.0" style launch with the State Cans feature | schedule a launch day; prep gallery from press kit |

### Tier 2 — Food / internet-culture newsletters (where the DC audience actually is)
| Target | Angle | How to reach |
|--------|-------|--------------|
| Snaxshot | Beverage-culture-native; "fridge cigarette" is exactly their beat | email/DM, lead with the culture not the app |
| Garbage Day (and similar internet-culture letters) | "There's now an app for the fridge cigarette" is a perfect one-line item | tips email; keep the pitch to 3 sentences |
| Local/regional food newsletters & morning shows | State Cans = a local hook for any state ("collect YOUR state's can") | pitch the state angle |

### Tier 3 — Diet Coke creators & meme accounts (seeding, not press)
- Search IG/TikTok for accounts that already post DC content organically: hashtags `#DietCokeTok`, `#dietcoke`, `#fridgecigarette`; 5k–50k followers is the sweet spot (they reply, and their audience trusts them).
- Offer: **free lifetime Pro via an App Store offer code** (one code batch per creator so you can see redemptions in App Store Connect), zero obligations. Soft ask: "if you like it, your weekly recap card is very postable."
- Goal: 3–5 seeded creators in month 2 (per the [90-day calendar](00-master-strategy.md#6-90-day-calendar)).

### Tier 4 — Podcasts
| Target | Angle |
|--------|-------|
| Indie-dev podcasts (e.g. Launched, Under the Radar–adjacent shows that interview devs) | Solo dev builds a gamified tracker for a meme; CloudKit/SwiftUI/Watch tech story |
| Snack/food-culture podcasts | The Diet Coke fandom phenomenon, with the app as the artifact |

## Pitch templates

Send from your real address (brevbot2@gmail.com or a fridgecig domain address if you set one up). Keep subject lines exactly this short. Personalize the first line — one sentence proving you read their stuff — or don't send it.

### Template 1 — Press / newsletter pitch

> **Subject:** The "fridge cigarette" now has an app
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> [One personalized line — e.g., "Loved your piece on X."]
>
> I'm a solo developer and, unfortunately, a Diet Coke person. So I built FridgeCig — a gamified tracker for Diet Coke superfans. You log every can (yes, McDonald's fountain is its own category, as it should be), keep a daily streak, unlock badges, collect a "State Can" from all 50 states, and share stat cards that are already all over #FridgeCig.
>
> It's native SwiftUI with widgets, an Apple Watch app, and CloudKit sync — no accounts, no ads. The tone is the point: "We don't judge. We just track."
>
> App Store: [press custom product page link]
> Press kit (screenshots, fact sheet, demo video): [link]
>
> Happy to share promo codes for you or your readers.
>
> [Your name] — developer of FridgeCig

### Template 2 — Creator seeding

> **Subject:** free lifetime Pro of the Diet Coke app (no strings)
>
> Hey [Name],
>
> Your [specific post] is exactly why I built this. FridgeCig is an app that tracks your Diet Coke habit — streaks, badges, a leaderboard, and a weekly recap card that's honestly made for posting.
>
> I'd love to give you lifetime Pro, free, no strings: [offer code redemption link]. If you end up liking it and ever feel like sharing your recap card or streak, amazing — but zero obligation.
>
> Either way, keep the DC content coming. 🥤
>
> [Your name] — solo dev, FridgeCig

### Template 3 — Podcast pitch

> **Subject:** Solo dev of the Diet Coke tracking app — guest idea
>
> Hi [Name],
>

>
> Happy to talk numbers, weird user stories, or the tech. App Store link and press kit: [links].
>
> [Your name]

### Template 4 — Follow-up (send exactly one, 5–7 days later)

## Reddit scripts

Reddit is outreach too — to the community directly. Rules from the [original doc](../social-media-content-strategy.md#reddit): be genuine, no hard sell, engage every comment for the first 2 hours after posting. Check each subreddit's self-promo rules first.

**r/dietcoke (Week 2):**
> **Title:** I built an app to track my Diet Coke habit. It has badges.
>
> **Body:** Long-time lurker. My DC habit got to the point where I, a developer, did the only reasonable thing: built a full tracker for it. Streaks, badges (there's one called "Send Help"), a friends leaderboard, and McDonald's fountain as its own category because we all know it's different. Screenshots attached. It's called FridgeCig — would genuinely love this sub's feature ideas, you people understand the assignment.

**r/IndieApps (Week 3):**
> **Title:** Solo dev — I made a habit tracker specifically for Diet Coke people, and the niche is the strategy
>
> **Body:** Instead of building the 1000th general habit tracker, I built one for a single beverage subculture. Gamification (streaks/badges/leaderboards), social feed, collectible "State Cans" for all 50 US states, share cards with the hashtag baked in. Monetized with RevenueCat subs + lifetime. Happy to answer anything about going ultra-niche.

**r/SwiftUI (Week 4):**
> **Title:** Built with SwiftUI + CloudKit: a Diet Coke tracker with streaks, social feed, Watch app, and widgets
>
> **Body:** No custom server — CloudKit public + private databases handle the social feed, leaderboards, and sync. SwiftUI end to end across iOS, watchOS, and widgets, plus App Intents for Siri logging. Ask me anything about CloudKit's sharp edges; I've found most of them.

## Press kit one-pager

Create this as a public page or PDF (e.g., add to the GitHub Pages site at brevinb.github.io/FridgeCig-Legal or a `/press` page) and link it in every pitch:

- **Fact sheet:** FridgeCig · gamified Diet Coke tracker · iOS + Apple Watch + widgets · by Brevin B (solo developer) · [launch year] · v2.2.0 · free with Pro subscription (streak freezes, widgets, Watch logging, themes, Health sync) · App Store link · contact email
- **The one-paragraph story:** the elevator pitch from the [master strategy](00-master-strategy.md#1-positioning)
- **Screenshots:** the 10-shot list from the [original doc](../social-media-content-strategy.md#screenshot--video-shot-list), generated with the debug screenshot seeder so they show a lively account
- **Demo video:** the 20-second "log → streak → badge → share card" cut from the content pack
- **Founder angle:** "Solo dev builds the app Diet Coke people didn't know they needed" — one short paragraph, first person, self-aware
- **Boilerplate quotes they can lift:** "We don't judge. We just track." / "It's Pokémon for people who love aspartame." / "McDonald's Diet Coke is its own category, as it should be."
- **Promo codes:** note that codes are available on request
