Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 10, 2026

FridgeCig ("we," "our," or "the app") is operated by Brevin B. This Privacy Policy explains what data FridgeCig collects, how it is used, and your choices regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

Account Data

  • Display name and username you choose during setup
  • An auto-generated friend code used to connect with other users

Drink Log Data

  • Drink type, brand, timestamp, volume, notes, and ratings you record

Photos

  • Optional drink photos you attach to log entries
  • Photos are stored locally on your device and, if you choose, shared to the social activity feed
  • All image processing (label recognition via Apple Vision) happens entirely on your device — no images are sent to any cloud service for analysis

Health Data

  • If you opt in, FridgeCig writes dietary caffeine data to Apple HealthKit
  • This is strictly opt-in; no health data is read or collected without your explicit permission

Contacts

  • If you grant permission, FridgeCig accesses first and last names from your Contacts to suggest friends
  • Contact data is used only on-device for matching and is never uploaded to or stored on any server

Device & Account Info

  • Your iCloud account is used for data sync and social features
  • We do not perform device fingerprinting or collect device identifiers

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide FridgeCig's features:

  • Personal drink tracking, streaks, and statistics
  • Badge and milestone calculations
  • Leaderboards and social activity feed (shared with your friends)
  • Weekly recap summaries
  • Caffeine logging to HealthKit (if enabled)

3. Third-Party Services

  • Apple CloudKit — Syncs your data across your devices and powers social features (leaderboards, activity feed). Data is stored in Apple's iCloud infrastructure under their privacy policy.
  • Apple HealthKit — Receives caffeine data if you opt in. We never share HealthKit data with third parties or use it for advertising.
  • RevenueCat — Manages subscription status. RevenueCat receives only the purchase information necessary to validate your subscription; no personal data beyond what Apple provides for the transaction is shared.
  • Apple StoreKit — Handles all payment processing. Apple manages billing, receipts, and payment information directly; we never see your payment details.

4. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not use analytics SDKs or tracking frameworks
  • We do not display ads or use ad networks
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing
  • We do not perform any cloud-based machine learning on your data

5. Social & Public Data

When you use FridgeCig's social features, certain data is shared with your friends:

  • Leaderboard stats (drink counts, streaks)
  • Activity feed items (drink logs, badges earned, milestones)

You control what is shared. Badges, milestones, drink logs, and photos are each independently toggleable in your sharing preferences. You can also set your profile visibility.

6. Data Storage & Security

  • Cloud data is stored in Apple CloudKit and protected by Apple's iCloud encryption
  • Photos are stored locally on your device and excluded from device backups
  • All image processing happens on-device using Apple's Vision framework

7. Your Controls

FridgeCig provides the following controls:

  • Visibility settings — Control who can see your profile and activity
  • Sharing preferences — Toggle what appears on the activity feed (badges, milestones, drink logs, photos)
  • Notification preferences — Manage push notification types
  • HealthKit — Enable or disable caffeine logging at any time via the app or iOS Settings

8. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app's settings. This will:

  • Remove your profile, drink logs, and social data from CloudKit
  • Remove locally stored photos
  • Remove your data from leaderboards and activity feeds

Caffeine data previously written to HealthKit must be removed separately through the Apple Health app.

9. Children's Privacy

FridgeCig is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of FridgeCig after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at brevbot2@gmail.com.
