# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 4, 2026**

FridgeCig ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Display name, username, and friend code for social features
- **Drink Entries:** Beverage type, size, timestamps, and optional notes
- **Photos:** Optional images you attach to drink entries
- **Preferences:** Your beverage preferences, theme selections, and notification settings

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Statistics:** Drink counts, streaks, and consumption totals
- **Device Information:** Device type and operating system version for app functionality

### Optional Health Data

If you enable Apple Health integration, we read and write dietary caffeine data to Apple HealthKit. This data:
- Remains on your device and in your personal iCloud account
- Is never sent to our servers
- Is never sold or shared with third parties
- Can be disabled at any time in Settings

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and maintain the app's core functionality
- Enable social features including friend connections and leaderboards
- Track your streaks and award achievements
- Sync your data across your devices via iCloud
- Process subscription purchases
- Send notifications you've opted into (streak reminders, friend activity)

## Data Storage and Sync

### iCloud (CloudKit)

Your drink entries, profile, and preferences are stored in Apple's iCloud using CloudKit. This means:

- Your data syncs securely across your Apple devices
- We do not have direct access to your personal drink entries
- Your data is protected by Apple's security infrastructure
- Leaderboard profiles are stored in a shared database to enable rankings

### Local Storage

Some data is stored locally on your device, including:
- Cached preferences for offline access
- Widget and Apple Watch data via App Groups

## Third-Party Services

### RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect:
- Transaction identifiers
- Subscription status
- Anonymous usage analytics

RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### Apple Services

We integrate with Apple services that have their own privacy practices:
- **iCloud/CloudKit:** For data sync
- **HealthKit:** For optional caffeine logging
- **Apple Push Notifications:** For notifications

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only:

- **With other users:** Your public profile (display name, username, stats) if you enable public visibility
- **With service providers:** RevenueCat for payment processing
- **As required by law:** To comply with legal obligations

## Data Retention

- Your data is retained as long as you use the app
- You can delete your account and data at any time through the app settings
- Drink entries can be individually deleted from your history

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access:** View all your data within the app
- **Delete:** Remove individual entries or your entire account
- **Opt-out:** Disable notifications, HealthKit sync, and public profile visibility
- **Export:** Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account

## Children's Privacy

FridgeCig is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

## Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest through Apple's iCloud infrastructure.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last Updated" date.
